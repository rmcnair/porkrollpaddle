The Pork Roll Paddle

A jaunt around the Garden State

The Why

Because it sounds cool! We're from NJ but haven't explored all that much of the state. What better way then to do it than a nice long paddle.

The Route

We'll start in Port Jervis, paddle down the Delaware, around Cape May, and then up the coast through the bays. We'll be walking boat around the Point Pleasant Canal, and likely most of the way to Long Branch (don't worry we'll have wheels).

The Dates

We're launching on August 9th and need to be done by September 8th (we have a wedding to get to... hopefully we won't be too burnt).

The Ask

There's not much camping along the way. If you live near the route and are open to letting some nice paddlers crash there for the night, please reach out. We're happy to camp in your yard and we promise to have some interesting stories :) If this is something you are open to, or you just want to chat, fill out the old form below and I'll reach out to you.

FAQ

You can't actually paddle around NJ.

That's not really a question, but fair enough. Yes, we'll be hiking the norther section (on the Appalachian Trail), about 50 miles. But the other 500 miles are bordered by water - pretty neat.
